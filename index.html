Book Recommendation App

Search for recommendations based on a book title or author name.

A couple tips to make your searches more effective:

  • Using an author's full name (first and last) is likely to give better results, as is being precise in spelling and use of special characters - for example, a search for Roberto Bolaño (note the ñ!) will do better than a search for Roberto Bolano!
  • If you don't find a review of a specific book, searching for that author is more likely to find a book review.

Did you find a specific book you're interested in? It might be freely available online at Open Library! To search, enter the ISBN for your book of interest.
