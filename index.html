Book Recommendation App

This app will provide book and author recommendations.

Enter at least 1, and as many as 3, books/authors/musicians/albums/TV shows/movies - anything you like!

Eventually, will include some "guidance" to help facilitate more-successful searches.






Did you find any specific books you're interested in? They might be freely available online at Open Library. To search, enter the ISBNs for any books you've found.
